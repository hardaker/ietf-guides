To Become a Guide

First off, thank you for being willing to help by participating as a Guide. Guides are advisors, coaches, and teachers that provide the guidance needed to assist IETF newcomers in developing the skills needed to participate effectively within the IETF standards process. For more information on the skills required, please see the FAQ for potential guides.
